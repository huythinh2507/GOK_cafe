# Product Images

Place your tea product images in this folder.

## Required Images:

1. **matcha-tea.jpg** - Matcha Green Tea product image
2. **bubble-tea.jpg** - Bubble Milk Tea product image
3. **fruit-tea.jpg** - Fruit Tea Special product image
4. **black-tea.jpg** - Classic Black Tea product image

## Image Specifications:

- **Recommended size**: 400x480px (portrait orientation)
- **Format**: JPG, PNG, or WebP
- **Background**: Light background (#E8DCC4) works well
- **Style**: Product photography with good lighting

These images will appear in the "OUR TEA COLLECTION" section on the homepage.
