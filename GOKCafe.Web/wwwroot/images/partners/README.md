# Partner Logos

Place your partner/brand logos in this folder.

## Required Images:

1. **partner1.png** - Partner logo 1
2. **partner2.png** - Partner logo 2
3. **partner3.png** - Partner logo 3
4. **partner4.png** - Partner logo 4
5. **partner5.png** - Partner logo 5
6. **partner6.png** - Partner logo 6

## Image Specifications:

- **Format**: PNG (with transparent background recommended)
- **Size**: Height should be around 60-80px, width auto
- **Style**: Logo/brand mark, high contrast
- **Background**: Transparent PNG works best

These logos will appear in the "OUR PARTNERS" section on the homepage.
