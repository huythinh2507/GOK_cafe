# Location/Store Images

Place your store/cafe location images in this folder.

## Required Image:

**store-front.jpg** - Image of your cafe/store exterior or interior

## Image Specifications:

- **Recommended size**: 800x600px or similar landscape ratio
- **Format**: JPG, PNG, or WebP
- **Style**: Professional photo of your cafe location
- **Content**: Could be:
  - Store front exterior
  - Interior of the cafe
  - Welcoming atmosphere shot
  - Map or location view

This image will appear in the "WHERE TO FIND US" section on the homepage.
